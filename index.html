Colors, generally

A few notes

  • Our red should be used exlusively for calls-to-action and links within the Crowdtap UI.

  • For now, that yellow is not used prominently throughout the UI. A derived version of it is used for the "in progress" action notification label. I suggest reserving this color for when nothing else works—as a Plan C, basically.

  • Try to avoid using a gray darker than #444.
